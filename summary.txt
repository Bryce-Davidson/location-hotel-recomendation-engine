The Vic, Ascend Hotel Collection is a modern and chic hotel located in a convenient location in Victoria BC. The hotel boasts clean, comfortable rooms with cozy beds and great bedding. The staff are friendly and attentive, ensuring that guests have a comfortable and pleasant stay. The hotel features a pub/comedy club below it, offering easy access to dinner options. Additionally, the hotel has a nice rec room with board games, Foosball, ping-pong, TV and couches for entertainment. However, some guests have reported minor issues such as the lack of clear instructions on how to dial reception or how to get an iron/ironing board in the rooms. There are also some concerns around safety in the underground parking lot. Overall, the hotel is recommended for its great location, cleanliness, and friendly service.
